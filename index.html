Slideshow Show

  • Slide 1: Title

    We could do virtually anything with this thing :D

  • Entirely CSS driven design

    The Plugin itself does nothing more than manage classes on each slide to denote active/inactive states. CSS handles all presentation.

  • Custom Slide Transitions

    This allows us to create custom slide transitions independant of JavaScript, however there will be a JS based fallback for browsers incapable of CSS Animations/Transitions.

  • Custom Slide Markup

    These slides can contain ANYTHING you want, such as clickable links, editable text, full slide images per the usual... I mean ANYTHING!

  • Markup Agnostic

    Put your slides in whatever makes sense. An OL for sequential slideshow of information, a UL if that's your thing, or let your DIVitis run WILD and DIV it up :P
